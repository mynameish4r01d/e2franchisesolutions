Vision

Empowering Global Entrepreneurs, One Franchise at a Time.

Mission

At E2 Franchise Solutions, our mission is to be the gateway to success for aspiring business owners worldwide. We provide a pathway to business ownership within the United States, offering a diverse portfolio of over 500 franchises. Our commitment is to enable investors like you to achieve their entrepreneurial dreams while enjoying the benefits of life in the dynamic and opportunity-rich USA. We facilitate legal residency, employment authorization for spouses, quality education for children, and higher education pursuits. With E2Franchise Solutions, you're not just investing in a franchise; you're investing in a brighter future.

E2 Franchise Solutions: Your Gateway to the USA Are you considering investing in a US business franchise and exploring opportunities in the United States? Look no further than E2Franchise Solutions! As your gateway to the USA, we offer a pathway to business ownership and a host of compelling benefits

OPERATE YOUR OWN
FRANCHISE BUSINESS

With E2 Franchise Solutions, you gain the opportunity to run your own franchise business within the United States. Whether you’re passionate about food, retail, or services, our portfolio of over 500 franchises provides diverse options to suit your interests and expertise.

LEGAL RESIDENCY FOR YOU
AND YOUR FAMILY

Investing in an E2 visa-qualified franchise allows you and your family to legally reside anywhere in the USA. Say goodbye to geographical restrictions and embrace the freedom to explore this dynamic country.

EMPLOYMENT AUTHORIZATION
FOR YOUR SPOUSE

While you are running your own business, your spouse can obtain an Employment Authorization Document (EAD), granting him/her the ability to work in any field he/she chooses. This flexibility enhances your family’s financial stability and opens up new career possibilities.

LEGAL RESIDENCY FOR YOU
AND YOUR FAMILY

If you have children below the age of 21, they can now attend US public schools from kindergarten through high school for FREE! This educational opportunity ensures their growth and development while you focus on building your franchise business.

HIGHER EDUCATION PURSUITS

Even if your children are 21 or older, they can still pursue higher education in the USA. Whether it’s college, university, or specialized training, the possibilities are endless.
